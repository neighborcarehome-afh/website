Our Story

Allowing Families to Become Family Again

At Neighbor Care Homes, we believe exceptional care requires continuous learning, accountability, and a commitment to best practices.

As proud members of the Adult Family Home Council, we stay connected to industry education, advocacy efforts, and evolving standards of care to better serve our residents and families.

We also utilize Synkwise electronic health records to support accurate documentation, medication management, care planning, communication, and regulatory compliance. This technology helps ensure our team remains informed and coordinated while allowing us to focus on what matters most - providing exceptional care.

What We Offer

Care Tailored to Every Individual

Each resident receives a plan built around their exact needs, rhythms, and preferences. Care that fits, not care that's convenient.

Dedicated, certified caregivers are available around the clock, providing attentive support, safety monitoring, and compassionate care day and night.

Every resident receives a personalized care plan tailored to their medical needs, daily routines, preferences, and goals.

Compassionate assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, transfers, and mobility while promoting independence and dignity.

Specialized support for residents living with Alzheimer's disease and other forms of dementia in a calm, familiar, and secure environment.

Professional wound monitoring and care focused on healing, infection prevention, comfort, and physician-directed treatment plans.

Respectful catheter management and monitoring to support hygiene, comfort, and overall well-being.

Freshly prepared meals and snacks served daily, with accommodations for dietary restrictions and personal preferences.

Licensed staff handle scheduling, administration, and physician coordination, no missed doses, no family worry.

Meaningful daily activities including music, crafts, games, gardening, exercise, and social engagement.

Common Questions

Answers to What Families Ask Most

We believe in total transparency. Here are the questions we hear most often, answered honestly.

How do I know if my loved one is ready for an AFH?+
Signs that it may be time include increasing difficulty with daily activities, safety concerns at home, loneliness or isolation, memory-related challenges, or caregiver burnout in the family. We offer free consultations to help you assess where your loved one is and what level of care makes the most sense.
Do you provide Dementia or Alzheimer's care?+
Yes. We have experience supporting residents with dementia and memory loss and strive to provide a safe, structured, and reassuring environment.
What's included in the monthly rate?+
Your monthly rate includes 24-hour supervision, medication management, assistance with activities of daily living, home-cooked meals and snacks, housekeeping and laundry, activities and social engagement, coordination with healthcare providers, cable, haircuts, and nurse delegation/assessments. There are no surprise add-on fees for standard care services.
Are visitors welcome?+
Yes. Families and friends are encouraged to visit and remain actively involved in their loved one's life. This is their home and the doors are always open. Families do not need to call or schedule visits but if you are hoping for management or staff to have time, we ask that families let us know in advance if you are wanting time set aside
What happens if my loved one's needs increase?+
We regularly review and update each resident's care plan as their needs evolve. Our licensed staff are trained to handle a wide range of care levels, including advanced memory care and hospice support. We partner with local healthcare providers to ensure continuity and always communicate proactively with families.
How do I start the admission process?+
It starts with a simple conversation. Call us or fill out the contact form and we'll schedule a free, no-pressure tour. We'll walk you through the home, answer every question, and if it feels like the right fit, we'll guide you through the assessment and move-in process step by step.
